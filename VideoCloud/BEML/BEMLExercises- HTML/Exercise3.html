Exercise: Style a video player

In this exercise, you will learn how to style the video player you created in the previous exercise using the Video Cloud Studio's Style editor.

Style a video player

  1. Login to your Brightcove Video Cloud account.
  2. Navigate to the Publishing module.
  3. Select All Players and locate the SimpleBEMLPlayer video player.
  4. With the SimpleBEMLPlayer video player selected, click Styles. In the Style editor, notice that there are two Editable Areas--Global and VideoPlayer. In this exercise, we will focus on editing the VideoPlayer styles.
  5. In the Editable Areas section of the Style editor, select VideoPlayer. In the Colors & Themes tab, you should see a list of components that you can style, including the Video, Player Chrome, Buttons, Time, Playhead, and Volume.
  6. In the Colors & Themes tab, expand the Player Chrome node and change the Gradient Top color to #999999 (light gray), the Gradient Middle color to #666666, and the Gradient Bottom color to #333333. You should see that the player chrome now has a darker gray gradient. Notice that the text and buttons are more difficult to see now.
  7. Expand the Buttons node and change the color of the Text and Icons properties to #FFFFFF (white).
  8. Expand the Time node and change the color of the Text to #FFFFFF (white).
  9. Expand the Playhead Well node and change the Buffered Gradient Top and Buffered Gradient Bottom properties to #333333 and #666666, respectively. You Should see that the player now displays a darker theme.
  10. Click Save & Close.
