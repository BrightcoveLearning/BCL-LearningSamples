Tip of the week: If you have a Professional or Enterprise account and want to take advantage of Apple's new HTTP Streaming protocol for delivery of video to iPhones and iPads, you will need to create some additional renditions for your videos. You can do this in Brightcove Studio in the Account Settings (remember that you need to be an Administrator on the account to do this. Go to Transcode Settings section and click the Add a Rendition button to add a new rendition. The table below shows the settings Brightcove recommends for four renditions for HTTP Streaming.
